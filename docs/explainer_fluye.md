# Fluye — AI-Powered ADHD Task Translator

**Product name:** Fluye
**One-line description:** An AI that turns your vague, overwhelming to-dos into concrete 5-minute steps — built for ADHD brains.

## Problem

People with ADHD freeze when faced with vague tasks. Writing "Fix the project" or "Start the report" gives you nothing to act on — traditional planners just pile on more overwhelm. The missing piece isn't organization, it's translation: turning a vague intention into a specific first step.

## Solution

Fluye asks 2-3 clarifying questions about your vague task, uses your personal work context (your role, your tools, your current projects), and breaks everything into 3-5 concrete steps completable in 5-30 minutes each. It works for technical work, personal goals, creative tasks, and everyday life. When you check off the last step, the task is done — no friction, no freeze.

## Tech
